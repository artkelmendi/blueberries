Step 1 — Inspect

Walked daily, checked twice

Agronomists walk every row of the estate. Soil, water and leaf are tested on a fixed schedule — thousands of inspections a season, logged bush by bush.

Step 2 — Select

Only the deepest blue

Berries are graded by size, firmness and bloom — that silver dust on the skin that says a berry was never touched twice. Anything less than perfect stays behind.

Step 3 — Export

Built for volume

Picked in the morning, chilled by noon, loaded by evening. Our cold chain moves pallets and tonnes — not punnets — delivering export-grade fruit to sellers across Kosovo and beyond, while the bloom is still on the berry.
